WELCOME TO THE
GUESS THE NUMBER GAME!

Test your logic. Beat your opponent. Guess the number!

HOW TO PLAY

  • 1. You will be matched with another player.
  • 2. A secret number between 1 and 100 will be generated.
  • 3. You and your opponent will take turns guessing the number.
  • 4. Throughout the game, both you and the opponent will receive feedback on your guesses ("too high" or "too low").
  • The player who guesses it first wins!
